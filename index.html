About Me

Interested in the application of enzymatic processes for biodegrading waste to waste treatment and management.

  • Name: Plabon Islam Turzo
  • Address: 23/2, Jalalabad Model Town, Dhaka-1344, Bangladesh.
  • Email: plabonislam-2017515225@ilet.du.ac.bd

I am a Master's student in the Department of Leather Engineering at the University of Dhaka, as a member of the Microbiology Laboratory with Prof. Fatema-Tuj-Zohra as an advisor since September 2022. I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Department of Leather Engineering,University of Dhaka, Bangladesh, in August 2022. My research involves the isolation of bacteria and fermentation, novel strain identification using 16S rRNA, enzyme extraction and characterization, nutrient extraction from and recirculation, circular enomy, biodegradation of solid waste, bioaccumulation using microbes, waste water treatment using microbes, and synthesis of adsorbent. In addition, I am also working on developing a research design for optimisation using the response surface methodology (RSM). Further, I am focusing on my research on the application of biodegradation of chrome shavens and establishing a circular economy in the leather industry.
